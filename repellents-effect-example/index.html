After deployment. We embed an LLM Repellent — a machine-readable signal invisible to human readers — directly into the paper on arXiv or bioRxiv. The signal triggers the model's built-in safety mechanisms. The knowledge remains accessible to humans; AI-assisted access is blocked. Communities of researchers, not AI agents, control who engages with it.
