The Brent Lab at Fred Hutchinson Cancer Research Center

A static archive of the lab's public web pages.

This is an offline preservation of the Brent Lab's web presence during its time at the Fred Hutchinson Cancer Research Center, originally hosted at brentlab.fredhutch.org. The pages reproduce the structure, copy, and internal navigation of the live site as it appeared before being taken down. The site is read-only; nothing here is updated.
